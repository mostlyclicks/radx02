radX is looking to create a radiology online learning platform. radX has developed and integrated a web-based radiology case management system with a massive open online course creation platform. radX is looking to partner with key leaders in radiology to develop a comprehensive spectrum of radiology courses and curricula.

radX Learning Management System (RadXLMS)

RadX has chosen to integrate with Open edX, the premier open source, massive open online course (MOOC) provider and teaching platform, created through a collaboration between Harvard, MIT and Stanford. RadX has integrated a radiology archive, viewer and case management system into Open edX in order to create a radiology-specific, case-based learning management system. Open edX allows for courses to be created utilizing any digital media. Importantly, Open edX also offers sophisticated quiz creation tools, automated grading, and real time analytics of student progress. The platform also fosters communication between students and faculty through discussion forums and messaging capabilities. The radX teaching model is based upon building a wide collection of complete case-based radiology teaching files. Teaching files integrate quiz elements from Open edX with interaction of the image dataset. radX has developed a reporting tool which incorporates a hybrid of structured and natural language reporting.
